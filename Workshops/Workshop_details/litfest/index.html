GENERAL RULES

  • There is no word limit, except for tweet a tale.
  • Multiple entries are accepted.
    In general, the best entry is considered, except for memeify, as mentioned.
  • Plagiarized entries will not be considered.
    If two entries are found to be the same, both entries will be disqualified.
    However, any other non-plagiarized entries from the same participant will be considered.



SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • You can email your entries to events.online@elan.org.in
  • Submit Your entries with the subject as the event name.
  • Your entry should either be a .doc or a .docx document.
